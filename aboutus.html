Ingredients

Our commuitment to excellence means we carefully select premium ingredients for our pizzas, guaranteeing a tase that exceeds expectations. Below is a list of the ingredients.

  1. Dough
    • Made from flour, water, yeast, salt, and sometimes olive oil.
  2. Tomato Sauce
    • A base sauce made from tomatoes, often seasoned with herbs like oregano, basil, and garlic.
    • Additional sauces may include pesto, barbecue sauce, Alfredo sauce, or buffalo sauce.
  3. Cheese
    • Mozzarella cheese, cheddar, parmesan, or provolone depending on the pizza.
  4. Toppings
    • Meats: Pepperoni, sausage, chicken.
    • Vegetables: Mushrooms, oninons, bell peppers, olives, tomatoes, and spinach.
    • Herbs: Basil, oregano, thyme, and parsley.
    • Olive Oil: Used for brushing the crust or drizzling over the pizza before or after baking.
    • Spices & Seasonings: Garlic powder, red pepper flakes, black pepper, and salt can be added for extra flavor.
  5. Other Ingredients
    • Truffle oil, fig jam, prosciutto, arugula, and goat cheese.
